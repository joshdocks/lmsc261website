Matt

Matt is a formerly world renowned mayonnaise instrumentalist that suffered severe head trauma in Stockholm, Sweden. Unfortunately, Matt has been left with amnesia and knows nothing but mayonnaise and music. Thanks to a nearby good samaritan, Matt joined MDMJ as their dedicated mayonnaisierre. Matt's burning memories of a previous life leave a mark on the music of MDMJ with songs such as Men Kissing and I was hit in the head with a Washing Machine.

Dooho

I'm a professional washing machine player who has been in demand as a studio musician since the late 1950's.

I'm an expert at getting people stuck in, and unstuck from, washing machines.

Born in 1784, I rolled out of a mountainside in Stockholm, Sweden with the sole purpose of becoming a professional washing machine player.

Since my birth, I have been dropping my washing machine from various balconies to achieve ultimate tone.

Josh

I started playing piano at a young age with a focus on classical music before eventually moving towards learning about jazz and its theory in middle school.
